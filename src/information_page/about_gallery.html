How to Use Gallery page?

At the top of the gallery page, you'll find a series of thumbnail images from the test dataset. To select an image for analysis, simply click on the corresponding thumbnail.

After selecting an image, the application will process it using the pre-trained neural networks. This may take a few seconds.

Once processing is complete, the page will display the original image, the annotated mask provided by the dataset authors, and the mask generated by the neural network. These will appear in order beneath the selection bar. Images are enlargeable by 10% on hover.

Below the neural network's mask, you'll find an expander titled "Performance Metrics". Click on this to view the performance metrics for the selected image in a tabular format.

To select a different image for analysis, click on another thumbnail from the top row. The top row of thumbnails is always visible and clickable, allowing for easy navigation and comparison.
