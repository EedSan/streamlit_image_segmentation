This page allows you to view the performance of neural networks on your own geospatial images. The system supports U-Net, SegNet, and DeconvNet architectures.

How to Make a Prediction?

  1. Click on the "Browse files" button and select the required image from your computer. Important Note: At the moment system can only process 16-bit TIF geo-images with a resolution of 256x256 pixels.
  2. After uploading the image, select the neural network architecture you want to use for prediction. You can switch between U-Net, SegNet, and DeconvNet by selecting corresponding model from dropdown menu.
  3. Wait a few seconds for the system to process the image and generate the prediction.

Results

The results consist of two parts: the image you uploaded, and an image mask highlighting the area where fire is detected, if any.
